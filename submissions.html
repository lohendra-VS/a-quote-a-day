Welcome! This guide will walk you through submitting your favorite quote to A Quote A Day!

Step 1 · Create a New Quote File

Click the golden button at the bottom of this page. GitHub will automatically:

  • Create your own copy (fork) of the project
  • Open an editor where you can add your quote
  • Guide you through submitting your contribution
New to GitHub? Don't worry! The process is beginner-friendly and you'll be guided through each step.

Step 2 · Name Your File Correctly

The filename must start with a date in this exact format:

YYYY-MM-DD_yourname_department.md

For example:

2026-02-04_meenz_cyber-security.md
Important: The date must be in YYYY-MM-DD format (year-month-day, with leading zeros). Files without proper dates won't be processed.

Step 3 · Add Your Quote Content
